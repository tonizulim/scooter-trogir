Our Story

More Than Just Rentals

ScooterTrogir started as a simple idea: make it easy for tourists to explore the beautiful Dalmatian coast the way locals do — on two wheels.

Based in Seget Donji, just minutes from Trogir's UNESCO old town, we're a family-run business that takes pride in offering clean, reliable scooters with genuine local hospitality.

Whether you want to discover hidden beaches on Čiovo, cruise the coastal road to Split, or explore medieval castles in Kaštela — we'll set you up with the perfect ride and the best insider tips.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver the scooter to Trogir? +
Yes — free delivery and pickup to Trogir, Okrug Gornji, Okrug Donji, Kaštela, Split airport and the surrounding area.
How much does scooter rental in Trogir cost? +
50cc scooters start from €29/day, 125cc from €44/day, and 250cc/300cc from €47/day. Weekly rates are discounted. All prices include helmets, insurance and free delivery.
What driving license do I need to rent a scooter in Trogir? +
For 50cc scooters a category AM or B license is sufficient. For 125cc and above you need an A1 or A category license. Minimum age is 18 years and you must carry your license with you.
Do I need a motorcycle license? +
For 50cc scooters, a regular car license (B category) is enough in most EU countries. For 125cc+, you need an A1 or A2 license.
Can you deliver to Split Airport? +
Absolutely. We deliver for free to Split Airport, Trogir, Okrug, Čiovo, and the Kaštela area.
What do I need to rent? +
A valid ID or passport, a valid driver's license, and a security deposit (cash or card).
